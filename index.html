Business intelligence services we provide

Innowise Group experts provide a wide range of BI development and BI consulting services, including custom development, BI support, and maintenance, creation of self-service solutions, etc. As a result, most of our clients have optimized their operational processes and gained new business perspectives. To get a bigger picture of what we do, read below.

Data visualization

Large amounts of data in different formats are difficult to process. We can help you create a solution that would bring all the business data into a single format. Afterwards, it can be easily processed and visualized.

Business insights and data analysis

We implement data analysis and help our clients make decisions based on facts instead of intuition. In practice, there is a strong difference between business decisions made on structured reliable data or grounded on some random insights.

DWH and ETL architecture

To store a giant amount of unstructured data and provide its simple and easy migration, we use DWH (data warehouse) and implement ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes for each project. Such a technical approach ensures efficient data storage and processing.

Custom BI solutions and components

Despite the abundance of ready-made business intelligence products on the BI market, it can be difficult to find one that meets your exact business needs. In this case, you can contact us to order custom development for a suitable solution.

Enterprise business intelligence

For big companies, we create scalable business intelligence solutions that can be upgraded and adapted to changing business needs and market trends over time.

Implementation of BI applications

Not only do we create, but also efficiently integrate BI solutions into existing systems.

BI consulting

If you have not yet decided on the technology stack for your BI solution or hesitate about its functionality and design, we will guide you through the best market practices.

BI support & maintenance

Existing BI solutions require timely updates and tech support. We can add new features and fix technical issues on the go.

Self-service BI

Benefits of business intelligence and analytics

When implemented correctly, BI solutions bring a range of benefits to business owners, such as increased organizational efficiency, reliable data, improved customer experience, and quality data analysis. Below, we will go through the most common benefits of the business intelligence approach.

Increased organizational efficiency

By processing real-time and historical data, BI solutions can significantly improve the accuracy of business development scenarios and allow companies to distribute their resources wiser.

Structured reliable data

Instead of allocating separate software tools for all dispersed data sources, business intelligence and analytics solutions bring it together, structure, and visualize for further analysis.

Happier customers

Once implemented, BI technologies and tools quickly detect any breaches in customer service and show ways to avoid them.

Quality and fast data analysis

BI platforms quickly process impressive amounts of data and allow business owners to make a quick search of the relevant information.
